==Trivia==
*{{BGMPage|bgm_map_Brave_01.ogg}} was played during the [https://fecipher.jp/event/tour2017/ Fire Emblem Cipher GO! GO! Summer Tour] 2017 mini concert music program played by the Fire Emblem's sound staff "cavaliers".
*{{#cargo_query:tables=BackgroundMusic|fields=Title|where=Filename='bgm_map_Brave_02.ogg'|group by=Filename|default=}} received a remix in [http://symphony-5.com/fe_tscm0081/ FIRE EMBLEM MUSIC COLLECTION : SESSION ～Flower of Enchantment～].
* {{#cargo_query:tables=BackgroundMusic|fields=Title|where=Filename='bgm_menu_normal.ogg'|group by=Filename|default=}} received an arrangement for Super Smash Bros. Ultimate by Hiroki Morishita, a composer at [[Intelligent Systems]]. The name of the song was stated to be revealed for 'the first time' as "Gear Up For...".<ref>Official Smash Bros. site, Retrieved 2018-08-21, https://archive.is/YGTz9</ref><ref>https://twitter.com/SmashBrosJP/status/1032074594499276800</ref>
* {{#cargo_query:tables=BackgroundMusic|fields=Title|where=Filename='bgm_menu_normal.ogg'|group by=Filename|default=}} also received an arrangement in Sounds of Fire Emblem from Cipher Festival – Galeforce CD.<ref>https://serenesforest.net/2019/01/29/sounds-fire-emblem-cipher-festival-galeforce-cd/</ref>
*Several songs, such as {{BGMPage|bgm_map_FE06_03.ogg}} and {{BGMPage|bgm_map_FE08_01.ogg}} differ from their original appearances in their origin games.
*{{BGMPage|bgm_event_serious3.ogg}} has much resemblance to the English folk song "Scarborough Fair", however no official record states whether this was intentional or accidental.
