==Trivia==
Some of the generic units that appear on this map represent possible classes of [[Lysithea]]'s allies in the Golden Deer: the {{Ut|Axe Flier}} being a stand-in for [[Claude]] (who is proficient in {{Mt|flying}} and possesses a Budding Talent for {{Wtt|axe}}s), the {{Ut|Bow Fighter}} for [[Ignatz]] ({{Cwtt|bow}}s), and the {{Ut|Blue Mage}} possibly for [[Marianne]] as she would be one in {{EntryText|0}}.
