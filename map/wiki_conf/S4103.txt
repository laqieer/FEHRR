== Trivia ==
The generic units that appear on this map represent possible classes of [[Claude|Claude's]] allies in the Golden Deer: The Lance Cavalier being a stand-in for Leonie (lances and riding), the Red Cavalier for [[Lorenz (Three Houses)|Lorenz]] (Reason and riding), the Axe Fighter for [[Raphael]] (axes) and the Axe Flier for [[Hilda (Three Houses)|Hilda]] (Axes){{Story Maps Navbox|book=4}}
